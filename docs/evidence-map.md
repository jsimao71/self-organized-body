# Evidence Map

| Claim | Status | Evidence needed |
|---|---|---|
| Higher levels constrain lower-level biology | Established systems principle, mechanisms context-dependent | Primary examples and formal models |
| Mechanical loading alters cellular signalling and tissue | Established | Mechanotransduction and exercise literature |
| Relaxation can reduce guarding/co-contraction | Plausible/partly established | Protocol-specific EMG and functional studies |
| Decades of practice restore original organization | Personal observation/hypothesis | Longitudinal standardized measurements |
| Ambiguous projects create multiscale adaptive cascades | Plausible hypothesis | Prospective psychophysiological studies |
| Improved health improves future improvability | Central testable hypothesis | Repeated-challenge experiments |
| Endogenous repair can exceed aging indefinitely | Speculative | Long-term human evidence across hallmarks |
| Lifestyle regenerates a severely damaged optic nerve | Unsupported | Direct clinical evidence would be required |
