# Book Ideas and Open Questions

- Is “How to Grow Young” best used as subtitle, final chapter, or provocative section title?
- Can adaptive capacity be measured as a second-order phenotype: the rate at which future adaptation improves?
- How should an organism-wide age distribution be defined mathematically?
- Which psychological identity constraints have the clearest physiological pathways?
- Can body-led practice be compared experimentally with directive physiotherapy?
- Which structural observations can be documented retrospectively?
- Is the healthy attractor one basin or a hierarchy of nested viable regions?
- What is the strongest tissue-specific impossibility argument remaining after cell-state plasticity is considered?
- How does cancer risk define the boundary of desirable plasticity?
- Can the framework be connected to morphological computation without overstating analogy?
